Track #1: Innerbloom by RÜFÜS DU SOL

I grew up listening to music of varied genres and languages and have developed a profound association with it. I wouldn't shy away from telling it has played an important part in my life. I might get a tattoo Music saved me to prove a point.

This series is an experiment: can the song in my headphones steer the words on your screen? Each post will name the track(s) I'm looping so you can hit play and read in sync. Think of it as sharing earbuds across the internet. Ultimately, setting the tone and theme for the rendering. This might not make sense on day zero but is one of those dear things I will continue to extend in future.

On a side note, I strongly believe people who introduce you new music are important and this is my attempt.

(Inspired by Ben Horowitz's The Hard Thing About Hard Things )
